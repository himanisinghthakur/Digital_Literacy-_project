# Social Media Do's And Don'ts Checklist

### 

### Do's



* ##### Stay aware of what you are  posting.
* ##### Use social media for positive communication.
* ##### Be genuine and let your real self show.
* ##### Protect your privacy settings.
* ##### Support and uplift people around you.
* ##### Respect others and their opinions.

##### 



### Don'ts



* ##### Don't post anything impulsively without thinking.
* ##### Stay away from spreading rumors or misinformation.
* ##### Refrain from using harsh or offensive language.
* ##### Never expose sensitive personal details online.
* ##### Do not engage in unnecessary conflicts or negativity.
* ##### Don't let social media distract you from what really matters in your life
