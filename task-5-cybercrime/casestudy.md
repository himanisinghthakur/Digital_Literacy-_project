Case Study: UPI / Online Payment Fraud

Introduction:-

Nowadays, many people use UPI for easy and quick payments. But along with convenience, online payment frauds are also increasing, where people are tricked into losing money.

Background of the Case:-

Aman, a 17-year-old student, had recently started using UPI for his daily expenses. One evening, he received a call from a person who said he was from the bank. The caller told him that his account could get blocked if he didn’t verify it immediately. Hearing this, Aman became worried.

How the Crime Happened:-

The caller smartly created panic and gained Aman’s trust. He sent a “receive money” request on Aman’s UPI app and told him to enter his PIN for verification. Aman didn’t realize that entering the PIN would actually approve a payment. As soon as he entered it, ₹10,000 was deducted from his account.

Targeted Victims:-

Such frauds usually target students, elderly people, and anyone who is not fully aware of how online payments work.

Consequences:-

Aman felt shocked and stressed after losing his money. He also became scared of using online payment apps again.

Conclusion:-

This incident teaches us to stay alert. We should never share OTPs or enter UPI PINs for unknown requests. Being careful can save us from such frauds.
